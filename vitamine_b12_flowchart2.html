Diagnostic Algorithm — Suspected Vitamin B12 or Folate Deficiency

Source: UpToDate  ·  Refer to laboratory-specific lower limits of normal

Suspected vitamin B12 or folate deficiency — ≥ 1 criterion:
  • Underlying condition or diet associated with deficiency
  • Macrocytic anemia with low reticulocyte count
  • Mild pancytopenia
  • Hypersegmented neutrophils
  • Unexplained neuropsychiatric abnormalities
